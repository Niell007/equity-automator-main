# South African Employment Equity Guide

## Overview

The Equity Automator is designed to help South African businesses comply with the Employment Equity Act (Act 55 of 1998) and its amendments.

## Key Compliance Areas

### 1. EE Reports

- EEA2: Workforce Profile
- EEA4: Income Differential Statement
- EEA13: Annual Report
- Succession & Development Plans

### 2. Designated Groups

- Black People (African, Coloured, Indian)
- Women
- People with Disabilities
- Implementation tracking
- Barrier analysis

### 3. Numerical Goals

- Equitable representation targets
- Workforce analysis
- Progress monitoring
- Implementation strategies

### 4. Documentation Requirements

- EE Plan
- Minutes of EE Committee meetings
- Consultation records
- Communication records
- Training records
